PAYMENT

A 50% deposit is required on all orders over $500 with the balance due upon the customer's inspection and satisfaction of the goods. The balance must be sent no later than 3 days from the date in which the order has been received. Interest may be charged for late payments at the rate of 2% per month, starting from the date shown on the invoice. All orders under $500 require full payment in advance.

  • Electronic Payments
    We accept payment using Paypal ,  which allows you to use the credit card of your choice. Once you decide on your order, I will send you an invoice via e-mail. It is as simple as any purchase you make with your credit card; once you confirm the amount is correct, enter the means of payment and other relevant information and click "Send." And your done. Safe and Secure.
  • Personal Cheques
    Your order will be processed once the cheque has been cleared.
  • Purchase Orders
    Accepted from institutions and government. Your order will be processed immediately upon receiving a P.O. number.

 

SHIPPING

Fill out the appropriate amount for shipping shown by the chart on the order form. These rates are for 5 to 7 business days delivery and may vary in price depending on where you live. If you require a faster delivery service, please notify me and a rate will be quoted.

 

TERMS & CONDITIONS

 

  
  • LOST OR DAMAGED GOODS

Notify us immediately if you have not received your order by the due date. Lost packages will be traced and re-shipped. Contact the courier immediately upon receiving damaged goods. Retain all the original packing; DO NOT throw anything out.
  • RETURN POLICY

    Custom made garments have a No Return policy. The item can be returned for altering with all shipping and handling costs the responsiblity of the customer.
    Stock sizes in the men's clothing may be returned after a 3 day inspection period for a refund or exchange with a 10% restocking fee. The item must not be changed or altered in any way - worn, soiled or damaged. The customer will be responsible for all shipping and insurance charges for returns.
