Accessories are an integral part to any man’s dress. History in the Making provides a number of styles of neckwear for the 19th and early 20th century gentleman, completing the costume. We use only the finest silks and linens to give a truly authentic look and to ensure many years of wear.

We are also provide a style of button-up spats (no elastics), with a leather strap and buckle that are perennial favourites, along with a variety of patterned or plain suspenders.

Please feel free to inquire after any accessory that is not shown here as we continually add to this collection as requests are made.
