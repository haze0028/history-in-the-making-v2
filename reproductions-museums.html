Reproductions: Museums

Sealer on Ice Flow, c. 1930
Canadian Museum of Civilization

History in the Making makes reproduction period clothing for use in museum display and for educational programs. We research the fabrics, styles and construction techniques to ensure the most authentic reproduction possible. We have provided costumes for many major Canadian and U.S. museums, as well as museums in Europe, Asia and South America.
